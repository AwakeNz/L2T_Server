Olympiad & Sieges Info
Olympiad Info

Olympiad Games are at Friday, Sunday and Saturday (6:00(PM) to 12:00(AM) - check it with .time command), new heroes on the end of each month. The enchant limit on olympiad games is unlimited. We allow only one character hero per person.
Castle Siege Info

Castle Sieges will be every week at sunday 4:00(PM).
Castles are ONLY accessible for ACTIVE clans with ACTIVE players, not for ALT clans, if your clan is not classified as ACTIVE, you wont be able to register.
